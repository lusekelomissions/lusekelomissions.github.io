About Us

Lusekelo Missions Skills Training Centre is a dynamic faith-based institution, a ministry of the Leadership Building and Empowerment Ministry (LBEM). Rooted in biblical principles, we equip individuals with vocational, spiritual, and leadership skills that enable them to live purposefully and serve their communities.

Our programs focus on hands-on training and discipleship, empowering youth, women, and vulnerable groups to become leaders and agents of transformation.
